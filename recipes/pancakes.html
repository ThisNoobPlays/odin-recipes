Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are flat, thin, and round cakes that can be cooked on a frying pan or a griddle. Serve with whatever toppings or fillings that you like. A good place to start is maple syrup and butter. This recipe is easy and takes 15 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
  2. Pour in milk, egg, and melted butter into the flour bowl and mix until smooth.
  3. Heat griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour batter on to frying surface in 1/4 cup amounts and brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot.
