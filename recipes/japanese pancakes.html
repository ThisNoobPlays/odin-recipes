Japanese Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are flat, thin, and round cakes that can be cooked on a frying pan or a griddle. These Japanese pancakes are fluffy and extra-thick and more spongy than regular pancakes. This recipe is done in 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs, separated into whites and yolks
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Pancake molds

Steps

  1. Combine milk and vinegar in a large bowl and wait 5 minutes.
  2. While waiting for the milk and vinegar, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in another bowl.
  3. In yet another bowl, use an electric mixer to beat the egg whites until stiff peaks of froth form.
  4. In the milk bowl, add the egg yolks and vanilla extract and mix well until combined.
  5. Pour the milk bowl into the flour bowl and use a whisk to combine everything.
  6. Add the mayonnaise into the flour bowl and stir until all lumps are gone.
  7. Fold in the egg whites at the end.
  8. Heat a griddle or frying pan over medium-low heat and melt some butter in it.
  9. Use the pancake mold and fill the batter up halfway.
  10. Cook each pancake until golden-brown on the bottom, then flip and cook the other side until golden-brown.
