3 Ingredient Pancakes

Description

These pancakes don't use any flour at all. The batter is based off of bananas and only needs 3 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/8 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl with a fork.
  2. Add eggs and baking powder to the bowl and mix the batter well.
  3. Heat a little butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Spoon the batter on to the skillet and cook until bubbles form and the edges are set.
  5. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.
